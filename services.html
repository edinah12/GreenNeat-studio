Our Services

At GreenNeat studio, we specialize in transforming your indoor spaces into lush, vibrant environments. Our curated selection of indoor plants includes everything from low-maintenance succulents to statement tropicals. Whether you're looking to purify your air, add a touch of green to your office, or create a relaxing home oasis, our plant experts are here to help you choose the perfect plants for your needs.

  • Wide variety of indoor plants (succulents, ferns, palms, and more)
  • Personalized plant care consultations and maintenance plans
  • Custom plant arrangements for homes and businesses
  • Professional plant delivery and installation services

Our in-house pottery studio offers a creative space for both beginners and experienced artists. Join our hands-on classes to craft your own unique pottery pieces, or collaborate with our artisans for custom designs. The studio is also available for private events, team-building workshops, and birthday parties.

  • Pottery classes for all skill levels (wheel-throwing, hand-building, glazing)
  • Custom pottery design and commissions
  • Studio rental for private events and group workshops
  • Wide selection of pottery supplies and tools for sale
